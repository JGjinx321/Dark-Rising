Joseph wakes up to a dark room. A single big back t.v. is loudly playing the 6 o'clock news. Joseph looks over at his digital clock through a fuzzy blur of just waking up. The red symbols display a time of 6:46 p.m. Joseph remembers passing out on the couch from an exhausting day at work. He groggily sits upright on his couch and reaches for the remote to turn the tv screen. "Heads of state are saying stay inside. Lock your doors, turn off any unnecessary lights, and try to keep noise to a minimal." The news anchor continues in her frantic tone. "They highly advise all residents to stay away from the outdoors and to try and keep any devices charged to receive regular updates from your head of state in case of emergency.". "Do not under any circumstance try and interact with the infected." If there is anyone you know who may be infected quarantine them imme-." The t.v. cuts off. Joseph begins to worry. He gets up and with a quickened pace and walks over to his window facing the street. Joseph peaks out behind his blinds and notices sparks shooting out from a cut power line whipping in the darkness on his blackout street. In the distance Joseph hears car engines roaring up and down streets and a single voice in the distance screaming what sounds like "Hurry!"
